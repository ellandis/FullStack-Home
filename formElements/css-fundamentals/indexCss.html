    Pseudo-class CSS
    :hover - Applies when the user hovers over an element. :focus - Applies when an element gains focus, such as when a user clicks on it or tabs to it. :active - Applies when an element is being activated by the user (e.g., being clicked).
    :visited - Applies to links that have been visited by the user. :first-child - Applies to an element that is the first child of its parent. :last-child - Applies to an element that is the last child of its parent.
    :nth-child(n) - Applies to the nth child of its parent (e.g., :nth-child(2) for the second child). :nth-of-type(n) - Applies to the nth child of a particular type of its parent. :not(selector) - Applies to elements that do not match the given selector.
    :disabled - Applies to form elements that are disabled. :enabled - Applies to form elements that are enabled. :checked - Applies to checkboxes or radio buttons that are checked.
    :empty - Applies to elements that have no children. :first-of-type - Applies to the first element of a specified type within its parent. :last-of-type - Applies to the last element of a specified type within its parent.
