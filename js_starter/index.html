Nothing is Free

There's no such thing as free because everything comes at a cost, whether visible or hidden. When something appears free, the price is often paid in other forms, such as time, data, effort, or opportunity. Companies might offer a "free" service but make money through ads, data collection, or future purchases. Even in nature, animals and plants must invest energy and resources to survive and thrive. Hence, the concept of 'free' is more of an illusion, as there's always an underlying exchange happening. Straight to the heart of it. What do you think?
